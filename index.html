About us



A-One Foreign Services is one of the best and distinguished agencies in the field of recruitment of all kinds of manpower(skilled, semi-skilled and unskilled) to work across industries - governmental and private sectors. Our office is approved & licensed by Govt. of India with License No.: (B-0718/DEL/PER/1000+/5/9088/2014).

Vision

To Be A Pioneer & Reliable Consultancy For Sourcing & Recruitment Of Skilled, Semiskilled and unskilled manpower

Mission

To Achieve A Highest Competence In Providing Recruitment Solutions For Employers & Employees

Values

Professional Temperament, Commitment, Transparency & Trust Between Employer, Employees and Us

Our office is situated at a prime location in the capital city New Delhi which is easily accessible from the international airport, near five-star hotel (The Suryaa), various government departments, foreign embassies & consulates, airlines, and well-known logistic support providers. Our office is fully equipped with all modern facilities for management and communication to pursue the workflow in a fast & continuous manner according to high quality standards.

CEO Message

A-one Foreign Services (AFS) is a dream company of the owner who had worked in the office of Saudi Ministry of Health based at New Delhi under the auspices of Royal Embassy of Saudi Arabia in India for more than 26 years on various responsible administrative posts. He has been instrumental in executing several key projects for recruitment of Doctors and Staff Nurses for Ministry of Health, Saudi Arabia.

A-one Foreign Services was founded with a goal of finding job opportunities for our fellow Indians to work abroad in all core industries with peace of mind and security like health care, oil & gas, Power and petrochemicals, infrastructure development and service industry etc

We aim to be a reliable partner in growth of overseas employers by providing them skilled & unskilled workforce chosen according to high standards of quality.

We execute all operations & projects efficiently with the help of our core team of highly qualified and experienced staff members to the best satisfaction of our foreign employers.

We are looking forward to building a successful relationship with our overseas employers & job seekers by providing them our best services.

HAMID ALI
Managing Director
